Accounts represent specific merchant environments within an Account Group. Each account has its own unique configuration for payment processing, checkout flows, and routing rules, while inheriting the organizational context from its parent group.

### Key capabilities

- **Account Lifecycle**: Create new accounts directly within a group to expand your merchant presence.
- **Organization-wide Visibility**: List and retrieve details for all accounts across your entire B2B structure.
- **Management**: Update account properties or disable accounts as needed to maintain your environment.
