Account Groups are the primary organizational units in Yuno's B2B structure. They allow you to group multiple merchant accounts under a single logical entity, which is ideal for representing business units, geographical regions, or specific partners.

### Key capabilities

- **Group Management**: Create and list account groups to mirror your business organization.
- **Resource Association**: Link groups with specific merchant IDs for streamlined operations.
- **Account Aggregation**: View all accounts belonging to a group in a single consolidated list.
