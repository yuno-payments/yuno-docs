In Yuno SDKs, **payment flows** and **enrollment flows** are separate. Payment is the process of completing a transaction; enrollment is the process of saving a payment method (e.g. a card) to a customer account for future use. This page explains both flows at a high level with diagrams and step sequences. Use the Quickstart guide and platform flow pages for implementation details.

### Payment flow steps

1. Merchant Server: `Create Customer` â†’ Yuno Server: `Creates Customer`
2. Merchant Client: `Initiate Checkout` â†’ Merchant Server: `Create Checkout session`
3. Merchant Server: `Create Checkout session` â†’ Yuno Server: `Creates Checkout session`
4. Merchant Client: `Initiate Checkout` â†’ `Initiate SDK` to continue payment flow
5. Merchant Client: `Initiate SDK` to continue payment flow â†’ Yuno SDK: `Receive checkout session`
6. Yuno SDK: `Receive checkout session` â†’ `List payment methods`
7. Yuno SDK: `List payment methods` â†’ User selects payment methods
8. Yuno SDK: User selects payment methods â†’ Callback with the `one-time token`
9. Yuno SDK: Callback with the `one-time token` â†’ Merchant Client: Receive `one-time token` (single use)
10. Merchant Client: Receive `one-time token` (single use) â†’ `Initiate payment`
11. Merchant Client: `Initiate payment` â†’ `Initiate SDK` to continue payment flow
12. Merchant Client: `Initiate SDK` to continue payment flow â†’ Yuno SDK: `Continue with the payment flow`
13. Yuno SDK: `Continue with the payment flow` â†’ Show screen for the user to complete payment
14. Yuno SDK: Show screen for the user to complete payment â†’ Display `payment result` (optional)
15. Merchant Client: `Initiate payment` â†’ Merchant Server: `Create payment`
16. Merchant Server: `Create payment` â†’ Yuno Server: `Creates payment` in the payment provider

### Enrollment flow steps

1. Merchant Server: `Create Customer` â†’ Yuno Server: `Creates Customer`
2. Merchant Client: `Add payment method` â†’ Merchant Server: `Create customer session`
3. Merchant Server: `Create customer session` â†’ Yuno Server: `Creates customer session`
4. Merchant Client: `Display payment methods to enroll` â†’ Merchant Server: `Request available payment methods to enroll`
5. Merchant Server: `Request available payment methods to enroll` â†’ Yuno Server: `Returns available payment method`
6. Merchant Client: `Display payment methods to enroll` â†’ Merchant Client: User selects payment method to enroll
7. Merchant Client: User selects payment method to enroll â†’ Merchant Client: `Initiate enrollment`
8. Merchant Client: `Initiate enrollment` â†’ Merchant Client: `Initiate SDK` to continue enrollment
9. Merchant Client: `Initiate SDK` to continue enrollment â†’ Yuno SDK: `Continue enrollment flow`
10. Yuno SDK: `Continue enrollment flow` â†’ Yuno SDK: Shows screens for the user to complete enrollment
11. Merchant Server: Receive `enrollment result` via webhook â†’ Yuno Server: Receive `enrollment results` from payment provider
12. Yuno Server: Receive `enrollment results` from payment provider â†’ Yuno SDK: Display `enrollment result` (optional)
