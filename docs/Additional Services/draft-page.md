Aida AI is integrated throughout the Yuno Dashboard, providing contextual assistance and AI-powered features directly within different dashboard sections. Aida AI helps you get instant answers, generate insights, and streamline your workflow without leaving the dashboard.

The Aida AI chatbot widget is available across all dashboard sections, including Payments, Insights, Routing, and more. You can access Aida AI at any time through the chatbot widget, which appears in the bottom right corner of the dashboard.

<Image border={false} src="https://files.readme.io/3c3a115c146bda27428dddd6d89e9c04207fea76ceaaa13140761dff2d3308f4-image.png" />

## Aida AI Chatbot Widget

The Aida AI chatbot widget is powered by Pylon and is accessible from any section of the Yuno Dashboard. When you open the chatbot, you'll be presented with the option to chat with **Aida AI** or a **customer support representative**.

<Image border={false} src="https://files.readme.io/b5a7251027abc7361ac8e0059a84e952f2e458ada60a3d0ddf60ad6a553183ea-image.png" />

**What you can do:**

* Chat with Aida AI for instant answers about payments, transactions, and platform features
* Switch to your account manager for personalized support
* Get help with technical questions, payment information, and troubleshooting
* Request assistance specific to the dashboard section you're currently viewing

**How to use it:**

1. Look for the chatbot widget in the bottom right corner of any dashboard section
2. Click to open the chatbot
3. Choose between **Aida AI** or **customer support representative**
4. Start your conversation and get the help you need

## Identifying Aida AI Features

Aida AI features throughout the dashboard are designed with distinctive visual elements to make them easy to identify:

* **Aida AI logo**: Look for the blue grid-like icon (Aida AI logo) in the chatbot widget header and within chat messages
* **Chatbot widget**: The chatbot widget appears in the bottom right corner of all dashboard sections
* **Clear option selection**: When opening the chatbot, you'll see a clear message asking if you want to talk with **Aida AI** or a **customer support representative**

## Getting Started

To start using Aida AI in the dashboard:

1. Navigate to any dashboard section (Payments, Insights, Routing, etc.)
2. Look for the chatbot widget in the bottom right corner
3. Click to open the chatbot
4. Choose **Aida AI** when prompted
5. Ask questions or request assistance in natural language

The chatbot widget is always available, so you can access Aida AI from any section of the dashboard at any time.

## What You Can Ask Aida AI

Aida AI can help you with a wide range of tasks across the dashboard:

* **Payment information**: Get details about specific payments, transactions, and payment history
* **Insights and analytics**: Understand your payment performance metrics and request custom visualizations
* **Routing configuration**: Get help creating and optimizing routing rules
* **Platform features**: Learn about Yuno's features, integrations, and capabilities
* **Troubleshooting**: Get help resolving issues and understanding error messages
* **Technical questions**: Ask about SDK integration, API usage, and platform functionality

For more information about Aida AI's capabilities, see the [Aida AI Agent documentation](/docs/aida-ai-agent).
