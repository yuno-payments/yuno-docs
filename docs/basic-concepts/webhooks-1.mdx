---
title: "Webhooks"
description: "Explains how Yuno webhooks notify your system of payment and event status changes."
---

A webhook is how Yuno notifies your system of events, like a payment being authorized or a subscription renewing, as an HTTP `POST` to a URL you configure, instead of your system having to poll for updates.

<Frame>![](/images/reference/webhooks-1/image1.png)</Frame>

See the [Webhooks Overview](/docs/webhooks) for delivery behavior, how to configure one from the dashboard or the API, and the full event catalog.
