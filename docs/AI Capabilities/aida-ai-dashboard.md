Aida AI is integrated throughout the Yuno Dashboard, providing contextual assistance and AI-powered features directly within different dashboard sections. Aida AI helps you get instant answers, generate insights, and streamline your workflow without leaving the dashboard.

## Using Aida AI across the dashboard

Aida AI lives in one chatbot widget (bottom right). Open it on any screen, choose **Aida AI** or **customer support**, and ask for help in natural language.

- Dashboard view: see the chatbot entry point in the bottom right.  
  ![Dashboard with chatbot entry point](https://files.readme.io/3c3a115c146bda27428dddd6d89e9c04207fea76ceaaa13140761dff2d3308f4-image.png)
- Chatbot choice: pick Aida AI or a representative when the widget opens.  
  ![Chatbot option to choose Aida AI](https://files.readme.io/b5a7251027abc7361ac8e0059a84e952f2e458ada60a3d0ddf60ad6a553183ea-image.png)
- Payments: ask about payment status, failures, history, or specific payment IDs.  
  ![Payments view with chatbot open](https://files.readme.io/676fb0d282e5d35c38723e912f0585427f47b17bb791f2badb756ae34ea2e877-image.png)
- Insights: ask for metric explanations, charts, or emailed visualizations.  
  ![Insights view with chatbot open](https://files.readme.io/75bda10ed0022a347965d53e30e065b3ae439769c3fa26ea27641524f9a4a8a8-image.png)
- Routing: ask for help creating, optimizing, or troubleshooting routing rules.  
  ![Routing view with chatbot open](https://files.readme.io/d20cd3b5f3673f63753d4559de31e381ab471bc6641b53e4f22e2cc654bf0ed5-image.png)
- Identifying Aida AI: look for the blue grid-like Aida AI logo and the bottom-right widget placement; you will always see the option to choose Aida AI or customer support.  
  ![Chatbot option reminder](https://files.readme.io/04f11017ad1d9cbf804e10fafdb2692e1acdd461363e046793babf548265d1e5-image.png)

How to use:
1. Open the chatbot widget in the bottom right.
2. Select **Aida AI**.
3. Ask your question.

## What you can ask Aida AI

Aida AI can help you with:

- Payment information: get details about payments, transactions, and history
- Insights and analytics: request explanations, charts, or emailed visualizations
- Routing configuration: get help creating and optimizing routing rules
- Platform features: learn about Yuno features, integrations, and capabilities
- Troubleshooting: resolve issues and understand error messages
- Technical questions: ask about SDK integration, API usage, and platform functionality

For more information about Aida AI's capabilities, see the [Aida AI Agent documentation](/docs/aida-ai-agent).
