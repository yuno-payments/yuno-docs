## Payouts

We have launched a new product that allows merchants to execute [Payouts](/docs/payouts) through the integration of various providers.

Yuno Payouts is an API solution designed to streamline the process of sending secure payouts to various beneficiaries, such as users, merchants, providers, and customers. With Yuno Payouts, you can efficiently distribute funds to multiple recipients while ensuring their security and taking complete control of your payout operations.

The Yuno Payout solution enables you to create payout requests without restrictions on the number of records. You can perform payouts to any bank, card, or wallet. The payout options are only limited by the provider you choose to use.

Please refer to the [technical documentation](/docs/payouts) for more info.

## Payment method fingerprint

We've added a new field to the [Payment method](/docs/enroll-payment-methods#step-5-retrieve-payment-methods) object called `fingerprint`. It is a field that represents your customer's card throughout your organization. When a customer enrolls a credit card multiple times related to one or many Yuno accounts, multiple `vaulted_tokens` will be generated, but the fingerprint lets you identify when the same card is used for the customer.

You will also find the `fingerprint` in the payment response when a transaction is made using an enrolled credit card.
