## v1.2.0
*March 10, 2026*

- <ChangelogBadge type="added" /> **PIX and Google Pay Support**  
  Added support for PIX and Google Pay as payment methods. Both are available immediately after upgrading with no additional configuration.

- <ChangelogBadge type="fixed" /> **Android 14 Crash**  
  Fixed a crash that occurred on Android 14 devices during SDK initialization. The issue was caused by a deprecated API removed in Android 14.
