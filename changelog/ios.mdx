# iOS SDK

All notable changes to the Yuno iOS SDK will be documented in this page.

## [2.15.0] - 2026-04-10

<ChangelogBadge type="changed" /> **Delegate Update**: Make `delegate.viewController` optional.
<ChangelogBadge type="added" /> **Apple Pay Contact Fields**: requests billing/shipping address, name, email, and phone based on required fields.
<ChangelogBadge type="added" /> **Shipping Address Support**: Support in card and APM forms with billing/shipping toggle and validation.
<ChangelogBadge type="added" /> **Auto-advance**: Auto-advance to the next field on valid input.
<ChangelogBadge type="added" /> **Glass Keyboard Toolbar**: iOS 26 keyboard toolbar with glass effect.
<ChangelogBadge type="fixed" /> **CVV Limit**: CVV field character limit for enrolled cards now applies the correct `securityCodeLength`.

## [2.14.0] - 2026-02-28

<ChangelogBadge type="changed" /> **Apple Pay Flow**: Move Apple Pay flow before OTT.
<ChangelogBadge type="added" /> **Free Trial**: Added free trial support.
<ChangelogBadge type="added" /> **New Localizations**: Add Hindi, Bengali, Malayalam, and Urdu localization with Urdu RTL support.

## [2.12.0] - 2025-12-05

<ChangelogBadge type="breaking" /> **Initialization Change**: Removed `cardFormType` from SDK initialization. Card form type must now be configured in the **Dashboard checkout builder**.
> See the [Migration Guide](/changelog/migration-guides/ios/v1-1-to-v1-2) for details.

---

## [2.0.0] - 2025-05-15

<ChangelogBadge type="added" /> **NuPay Installments**: Added support for NuPay installments.
<ChangelogBadge type="changed" /> **Flexible Actions Migration**: Migrated old Alternative Payment Methods (APMs) to flexible actions.
<ChangelogBadge type="added" /> **Mercado Pago 3DS**: Integrated Mercado Pago 3DS for enhanced fraud protection.
