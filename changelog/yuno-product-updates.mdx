## 1. New features and enhancements

Discover how Yuno's latest product launches, integrations, and feature updates are transforming payment operations.

### Payout product launch

Streamline global payout operations with **Payout**, a product designed to reduce complexity and unify processes through a single API. With Payout, you can:

* Access multiple payout providers and methods globally.
* Manage pay-ins and payouts on one platform for reduced maintenance and faster time to market.
* Lower integration and transaction processing costs.

Learn more about Payout on our [product page](https://www.y.uno/product/payouts).

### Smart Routing enhancements

Optimize your payment processing with **Smart Routing**, a feature that uses data-driven intelligence to improve approval rates and reduce costs. Key capabilities include:

* Geolocation and payment type-based routing for precise performance optimization.
* A/B testing to evaluate different routing strategies in real time.
* Customizable priorities to tailor routes by conversion rate, latency, or cost.

Discover how Smart Routing enhances payment performance in our [detailed post](https://www.y.uno/post/monitors-and-smart-routing-better-payment-performance).

### Monitors for payment flow stability

Introducing **Monitors**, a tool to ensure seamless payment operations by detecting anomalies and rerouting transactions to backup providers during critical events. Benefits include:

* Real-time anomaly detection and auto-routing.
* Customizable alerts and detailed insights in Audit Logs.
* Minimized revenue loss and enhanced reliability.

Learn more about Monitors on our [Smart Routing page](https://www.y.uno/product/smart-routing).

### Split Marketplace functionality

Simplify marketplace transactions with **Split Payments**, which allow merchants to divide payments among multiple recipients. With Split Payments, you can:

* Define amounts, recipients, and fees to suit your business needs.
* Streamline marketplace payment operations with greater flexibility.

Explore the feature in our [Split Payments documentation](/docs/payment-features/split-payments-marketplace).

### Required Fields in checkout

Enhance compliance and user experience with the **Required Fields** feature in the checkout builder. This functionality allows you to:

* Customize mandatory fields for each payment method.
* Streamline user interactions and meet provider requirements.

Learn how to configure Required Fields in our [Checkout Builder guide](/docs/using-yuno/dashboard-overview/checkout-builder#required-fields).

### SDK enhancements

Yuno introduces two new SDKs to simplify integrations:

* **Flutter SDK**: Makes development easier for Flutter-based applications.
* **Seamless SDK**: Streamlines the payment process with a single SDK call and API request. Learn how to use it in our [Seamless SDK documentation](/docs/sdks/full-checkout/web-payments).

## 2. Expanded Coverage and Integrations

Discover Yuno’s expanded global reach with over 20 new integrations, empowering businesses to leverage localized payment options.

### New integrations: Payment Methods and Providers

We’ve added integrations to support diverse payment methods across key markets:

* **Bank Transfers**:
  * Nuvei with Daviplata and PSE in Colombia
  * Inswitch in the Dominican Republic
* **Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL)**:
  * Koin Pix Parcelado in Brazil
* **Digital Wallets**:
  * Pluxee in Brazil, Colombia, and Panama
  * Mercado Pago with PSE Avanza in Colombia
* **Payment Links**:
  * Xendit QRIS in Indonesia
* **Card Payments**:
  * Fintoc in Chile and Mexico
  * Inovio in the United States
* **Other Payment Methods**:
  * Pinbank with Pix in Brazil
  * Nuvei with Nequi and Efecty in Colombia

These integrations, including expanded Payout coverage with **PayPal**, **Ecommpay**, and **Unlimint**, provide businesses with greater flexibility. Tokenization of card data through PayPal enhances both security and efficiency.

Explore all available integrations on our [partners page](https://www.y.uno/partners).

## 3. Dashboard updates

Explore the latest enhancements to Yuno’s dashboard, designed to improve transaction management, performance tracking, and user experience.

### Generate receipts for canceled transactions

Easily request receipts for canceled transactions, providing confirmation that the transaction was successfully communicated to the provider.

### New performance and latency charts

Track and analyze payment trends with new visual tools:

* **Today's Performance Review Chart**: Compare metrics like total sales, successful payments, and conversion rate between today and yesterday for quick insights.
* **Provider Latency Trends Chart**: Monitor provider response times at the P99 level, offering detailed performance insights over time.

Discover more about these tools on our [Insights page](https://www.y.uno/product/insights).

### Enhanced routing condition set reordering

Effortlessly reorder routing condition sets with improvements to both tree and list views. This update simplifies and accelerates the process of prioritizing conditions, enhancing the [user experience for Smart Routing](https://www.y.uno/product/smart-routing).

### Decline groups across multiple accounts

Create and reuse **Decline Groups** across multiple accounts, streamlining the setup and configuration of routing strategies. Save time by avoiding duplication and enhancing efficiency during configuration.
